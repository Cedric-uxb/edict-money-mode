Install on your phone / 安装到手机

CalmPlan is currently a PWA-style web app. After it is deployed to a public HTTPS link, you can save it to your phone's home screen.

iPhone / iOS

  1. Open the CalmPlan link in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add.

Android

  1. Open the CalmPlan link in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu button.
  3. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm the install.
