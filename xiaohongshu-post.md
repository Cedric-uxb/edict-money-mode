# CalmPlan Xiaohongshu Post

## Caption - Chinese

你有没有那种时刻：脑子里同时装着作业、投简历、邮件、面试、房租和一堆生活小事，结果越想越不想开始？

我做了一个小工具叫 CalmPlan。

它不是让你“变自律”，而是帮你把脑子里的混乱拆成：

- 今天先做的 3 件事
- 可以推迟的事
- 可以先放下的焦虑
- 一个 30 分钟行动计划
- 一段可以发给老师/客户/雇主的消息草稿

现在还是 MVP，但手机浏览器可以用。之后我会继续把它做成更适合学生、找工作、自由职业者的小工具。

如果你经常任务太多、压力太大、但不知道从哪里开始，可以试试这个思路：先不要规划人生，先规划 30 分钟。

如果你试了，欢迎评论区告诉我三件事：哪一部分有用、哪一部分没用、如果做成手机 app 你最想加什么。

## Caption - English

Ever feel like your brain is holding homework, job applications, emails, interviews, rent stress, and random life tasks all at once?

I built a small tool called CalmPlan.

It turns a messy brain dump into:

- 3 things to do today
- tasks to park for later
- worries to release for now
- a 30-minute action sprint
- a message draft you can send to reduce pressure

It is still an MVP, but it works in a mobile browser. I am building it for students, job hunters, freelancers, and overwhelmed knowledge workers.

The idea is simple: do not plan your whole life first. Plan the next 30 minutes.

If you try it, I would love feedback on three things: what helped, what felt useless, and what feature you would want if this became a phone app.
