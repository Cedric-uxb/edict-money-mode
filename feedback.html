CalmPlan

Feedback / 反馈

CalmPlan is still a small MVP. If you tried it from Xiaohongshu or a shared link, these three questions are the most useful.

中文

  1. 你输入脑内清单之后，生成的“今天先做”有没有真的帮你开始？
  2. 你觉得最没用、最奇怪或最想删掉的部分是什么？
  3. 如果它变成手机 app，你希望它多一个什么功能？

可以直接复制的反馈格式

我试了 CalmPlan。最有用的是：____。最没用的是：____。如果继续做，我希望加：____。

English

  1. After entering your brain dump, did the “Do today” section help you start?
  2. What felt least useful, confusing, or worth removing?
  3. If this became a phone app, what one feature would you want next?

Copyable format

I tried CalmPlan. The most useful part was: ____. The least useful part was: ____. If you keep building it, I would want: ____.

You can send this feedback wherever you found the app link, such as the Xiaohongshu post comments or direct message.
