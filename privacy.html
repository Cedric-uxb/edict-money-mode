CalmPlan

Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: June 5, 2026

English

CalmPlan is a small planning tool that turns a user's brain dump into a short action plan. The current MVP runs in the browser and does not require an account.

CalmPlan does not send your brain dump, tasks, worries, or generated plan to a server. Your input is processed locally in your browser. If you copy or share your plan, that action is controlled by you.

CalmPlan may store your language preference, current brain dump, and most recent generated plan in your browser's local storage so the app can restore your work on the same device. You can clear this through the app's Clear button or your browser settings.

CalmPlan is a productivity and planning tool. It is not medical advice, therapy, crisis support, or a replacement for professional help.

中文

CalmPlan 是一个小型计划工具，可以把用户输入的脑内清单整理成简短行动计划。当前 MVP 在浏览器中运行，不需要账号。

CalmPlan 不会把你的任务、焦虑内容或生成计划发送到服务器。输入内容会在你的浏览器本地处理。如果你复制或分享计划，那是由你自己控制的操作。

CalmPlan 可能会在浏览器本地存储你的语言偏好、当前脑内清单和最近一次生成的计划，用来在同一台设备上恢复你的内容。你可以通过应用里的“清空”按钮或浏览器设置清除。

CalmPlan 是效率和计划工具，不是医疗建议、心理治疗、危机干预，也不能替代专业帮助。
